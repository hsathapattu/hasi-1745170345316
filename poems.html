My Poetry Collection

Whispers of the Wind

Gentle breeze, a whispered song,
Carrying secrets all day long.
Through leaves and grass, it softly sings,
Of distant lands and wondrous things.

Published: January 15, 2024

Digital Dreams

In circuits and silicon, dreams take flight,
Bits and bytes paint worlds so bright.
A tapestry of ones and zeroes weave,
Realities that we can scarce believe.

Published: March 3, 2024

Echoes in the Code

In the silence, the code speaks clear,
A language built of hope and fear.
Each line a step, a bridge to cross,
Between the known and the unknown's gloss.

Published: May 10, 2024
